Full bare-metal repaint on a '73 Porsche 911 RSR
AK
Alex Kowalski
@alexk_builds · 214 posts
+ Follow
1973 Porsche 911 RSR — front 3/4
1 / 8
Year
1973
Engine
2.8L flat-6
Project time
14 months
Total cost
~$62,000

When I first saw this car sitting in a barn in rural Bavaria, it was barely recognisable. Three layers of mismatched paint, a cracked windscreen, and surface rust that had crept under every panel seam. But underneath all of that? Matching numbers. Original 2.8 litre flat-six.

"The moment the blaster revealed that original Tangerine Orange beneath three coats of grey — everything clicked."

We spent six weeks on metalwork alone. Every dent was worked out from the inside using traditional panel-beating techniques — no filler until the metal sat within 0.5mm of perfect.
